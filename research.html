Current Research

Kenya Healthcare Inequality Analysis (DHS Data)

This ongoing research project investigates disparities in healthcare access across wealth levels, education categories, and rural versus urban populations using the Kenya Demographic and Health Survey (DHS). The study combines statistical analysis, visualization, and regression modeling to better understand factors influencing healthcare utilization.
